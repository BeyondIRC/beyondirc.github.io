Welcome to BeyondIRC

We are a (now) small IRC network which as been around since January, 1997. While the original founders have moved on, we have kept the spirit of BeyondIRC alive for nearly 2 decades.

Servers

Currently, we have 2 servers for users to connect to:

  • New York City, New York, USA : walle.beyondirc.net
  • Kalispell, Montana, USA : whipcrack.beyondirc.net

However, we prefer that you connect to irc.beyondirc.net . This will ensure you can easily reconnect should we have a problem with one of our servers. All servers listen on ports 6660-6669 (plaintext) and port 7443 (ssl)

We also have a third server, hub.beyondirc.net, which is hosted in Amazon's Ashburn, Virginia datacenter. This forms the backbone of our network; no user connections are allowed here.
